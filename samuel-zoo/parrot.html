Parrots are part of the Psittacidae family and have been kept as pet birds since the ancient times because of their intelligence and for many, their colorful appearance. Parrots eat seeds, buds and also some insects and fruits. These birds are most well known for being able to talk by mimicking human speech, which they do naturally by learning and repeating sounds from their family or flock mates.
